Reignwood Group, headquartered in Beijing, China, is a global Chinese enterprise and brand active in four major growth sectors including Consumer Products, Lifestyle, Industrial and Financing. With sixteen industries and over sixty subsidiaries worldwide, Reignwood Group is leading its expansion and diversification with a commitment to a green, healthy, quality of life development concept and through the promotion of business and cultural exchange between China, Asia and the West as a key driver of Reignwood’s vision and mission.

Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts is dedicated to perfecting the travel experience through continual innovation and the highest standards of hospitality. From elegant surroundings of the finest quality, to caring, highly personalized 24-hour service, Four Seasons embodies a true home away from home for those who know and appreciate the best. The deeply instilled Four Seasons culture is personified in its employees - people who share a single focus and are inspired to offer great service. Founded in 1960, Four Seasons has followed a targeted course of expansion, opening hotels and residences in major city centres and desirable resort destinations around the world. With 92 properties in 38 countries, and more than 60 projects under development, Four Seasons continues to lead the hospitality industry with innovative enhancements, making business travel easier and leisure travel more rewarding.

Moinard founded his own eponymous 4BI Studio in 1996, and has since collaborated with illustrious brands such as Hermés, Chateau Latour and Veuve Clicquot, and has most recently been instrumental in the redesign of the Plaza Athénée, Paris
Aukett Swanke is a leading international firm of architects formed by the recent merger of two creative studios – Aukett Fitzroy Robinson and Swanke Hayden Connell Europe. With an impressive combined pedigree of over 90 years, the practice boasts a network of more than 340 staff across eight countries: UK, Germany, Russia, Turkey, UAE, Czech Republic, Brazil and Colombia. The enhanced experience profile within the studios now includes master planning, historic building conservation and restoration, architecture and interior design in the mixed-use, commercial office, hotels, residential, education and healthcare sectors as well as workplace consulting. Key hospitality and historic restoration projects include The Adelphi, The Lanesborough Hotel, Sanderson & St. Martin’s Lane Hotels and The Royal Exchange.

In March 2015 Reignwood Group and the London Symphony Orchestra announced a new partnership for 2015/16. Reignwood has become a Major Sponsor of the London Symphony Orchestra, supporting the Orchestra's broad range of work and in particular the ground-breaking digital education and outreach initiative LSO Play.
The new partnership with The London Symphony Orchestra reinforces Reignwood Group’s commitment to developing international cultural exchange between the UK and China and further fortifies Reignwood Group’s continued dedication to advocating the vital and enjoyable role that music, the arts and local community play in daily life. Dr Chanchai, Chairman of Reignwood Group, said 'Partnering with the LSO further promotes my belief that trust and communication can be enhanced through cultural exchange.'
